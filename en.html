SELF CHECK-IN/OUT GUIDE

Use the kiosk machine at entrance

• Use self check-in/out kiosk at entrance

• Staff assistance: 09:00 AM ~ 11:00 PM

• No staff available after 11:00 PM

HOW TO CHECK-IN

Step 01. Select Language

English

Step 02. Select "Check-in"

Step 03. Search your Booking

Search using either name or booking number:

Option 1: Search by First name or Last name or Full name

Example:

First name: JOHN Last name: SMITH

*Enter 4 more letters

Option 2: Search by Last 4 Digits of Reservation Number

Example:

Agoda: XXXXXX5735 Booking: XXXXXX5735

Step 04. Verify and Get Key Card

  1. Check if your name is correct
  2. Press "Next" to receive your key card
  3. Check your Room Number on screen and receipt

KEY CARD USAGE

• Use to open entrance glass door

• Use to unlock your room door

IMPORTANT

• Key card loss: KRW 10,000 charge

• Lost after 11 PM: Check-in next morning
